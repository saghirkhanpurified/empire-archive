Right now, 100% of your academic life—your syllabi, your reading materials, and your assignments—is trapped inside your university's centralized Learning Management System (LMS) like Canvas or Moodle.

**The day you graduate, they cut your access. You lose everything.**

### Building a Private Intelligence Network
The future belongs to **Decentralized Education**, an approach where learning resources are distributed across your own digital platforms rather than confined to a single institution. You must build a "Second Brain."

By leveraging software like Notion, you can extract your knowledge from the university's walled garden and build a personal, searchable database. 
*   **Data Sovereignty:** You own your notes forever. 
*   **Peer-to-Peer Routing:** You can instantly share customized databases with study groups without relying on a professor's permission.

Don't rent your intellectual capital from your university. Own it.
