# How to Monopolize Your University's Information Flow

Most students treat the role of **Class Representative (CR)** as a chore or a simple administrative task.

They are missing the tactical reality: The CR is the **Primary Node** in a university's information architecture. If you control the flow of data, you control the consensus of the group.

### Establishing Local Protocol Governance
To lead effectively, you must move away from disorganized WhatsApp chats and move toward a **Centralized Information System**. By utilizing advanced tools like **Notion**, you can create a "Source of Truth" for your cohort.

### The Tactical Sequence
1.  **Centralize the Database:** Build a Notion workspace that tracks every deadline, lecture link, and exam resource.
2.  **Information Asymmetry:** Become the person who knows the schedule before the professors even announce it. 
3.  **Consensus Management:** Use secret ballots and formal "votes of confidence" to ensure the group operates as a single, unified block during negotiations with the administration.

**Intelligence Verdict:** Don't just represent your class. Engineer the system they rely on to survive.
