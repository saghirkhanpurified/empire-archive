Corporate recruiters and Web3 startups are facing a massive talent bottleneck in 2026. Despite a flood of applications, qualified candidates are incredibly scarce. 

Why? Because a high GPA does not prove you can build anything. Companies are actively hunting for candidates with **live deployment experience**.

### Documenting the Process
To hack the modern hiring algorithm, you need a "Proof-of-Work" degree. You must document your process over time and provide verifiable evidence of your skills. 

*   **Deploy, Don't Tell:** Instead of listing "Next.js" on your resume, link to a live web application you deployed. 
*   **Public Ledgers:** Write public intelligence reports that solve real problems for other students. 

The market doesn't care what classes you sat through. It only cares what you have shipped to the internet.
