## Trust No One, Verify Everything

In modern cybersecurity, the old model of "castle and moat" is dead. Once an attacker breaches the outer wall, they have free rein over the internal network. 

Zero Trust assumes the network is already compromised. 

### Core Principles
* **Continuous Verification:** Always authenticate, authorize, and continuously validate security configuration.
* **Blast Radius Minimization:** Segment networks to prevent lateral movement.
* **Context Collection:** Collect behavioral data to respond to anomalies.

> "Never trust, always verify."
